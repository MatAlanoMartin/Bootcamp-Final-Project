Mat Alano-Martin spent 11 years
on the road telling jokes to
people all across the country,
and occasionally beyond.
Then 2020 happened.

(You may have heard about it)

In the before times, Mat appeared
at comedy clubs, theaters, punk rock dives, redneck roadhouses, casinos, colleges, living rooms, nudist colonies
and everywhere in between.
He hopes to one day do so again.

(Please wear a mask)

Mat’s albums Profiled as Such and
Blood Mouth can both be heard in regular rotation on Sirius/XM’s comedy channels and he has appeared several times on the nationally syndicated radio program
The Bob & Tom Show. AnD Also once on Laughs on FOX.

(You probably didn't see that one)

He has been lucky enough
to work with such notable acts
as Patton Oswalt, Tig Notaro,
Maria Bamford and Marc Maron
and has performed at over
20 different comedy, music and
fringe festivals here and abroad.
